Which Tunnel Shall You Proceed Down?

You're an underground mine worker and you come across a main room with three tunnels.
You can't go back the way you came because the ceiling collasped.
Down the left-most tunnel, you hear an undistictive animal noise -- it's loud and vicious.
Down the middle tunnel, you see a bright light blinding your eyes.
And down the right-most tunnel, you hear rushing water.
Keep in mind, the incline of the tunnels are so steep that once you go down, you can't come up.

What tunnel will you go down in?
